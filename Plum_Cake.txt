


My Grandmother Atkeson's Plum Cake

1  cup oil
2  cups sugar
1  tsp cloves
1  tsp cinnamon
2  cups self-rising flour
3  eggs
2  jars strained junior size plum babyfood
1  cup chopped nuts (pecans)
1  cup raisins (optional)

Mix all except nuts.  Add nuts and mix.  Bake at 325 degrees in a
greased and floured tube pan for approximately 1.5 hours.  Cool in
pan for 15 minutes before removing.
